Pepperoni Pizza

Pizza is a dish loved by many. Throughout the US, Pizza is consumed by many. Pizza is one of my favorite dishes. My favorite pizza place as a child was congress pizza in Chicago. The best type of Pizza is without a doubt Pepperoni pizza. The least consumed pizza is Pineapple Pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • olive oil
  • toppings of choice (Pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, etc)

How to make

Dough

  1. proof the yeast
  2. make and knead the pizza dough
  3. let the dough rise

Preparing the pizza

  1. Preheat pizza stone
  2. divide the dough into two balls
  3. prep toppings
  4. flatten dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  5. brush dough top with olive oil
  6. sprinkle pizza peel with corn meal, put falteened dough on top
  7. spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  8. Sprinkle cornmeal on pizza stone, slide pizza onto pizza stone in oven
